Required Documents for Services

Aadhar Card
  • Proof of Identity (Passport, PAN, Voter ID)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Rent Agreement)
  • Birth Certificate / Date of Birth Proof
Voter ID
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar Card, PAN)
  • Proof of Residence (Electricity Bill, Ration Card)
  • Passport-size Photo
PAN Card
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar, Passport)
  • Proof of Address (Bank Statement, Utility Bill)
  • Passport-size Photo
Passport
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID)
  • Birth Certificate / School Certificate
  • Passport-size Photographs
Driving License
  • Age Proof (Birth Certificate, Passport)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Rent Agreement)
  • Passport-size Photo
Ration Card
  • Proof of Residence (Electricity/Water Bill)
  • Family Photo ID (Birth Certificate, Aadhar)
  • Passport-size Photo
Tax Filing
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Statements
  • Income Proof (Salary Slip, Form 16)
Utility Bill
  • Previous Bill Copy
  • Meter ID / Account Number
  • ID Proof (Aadhar, Passport)
Pension
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Retirement / Service Proof
Vehicle Registration
  • Vehicle Purchase Invoice
  • Insurance Certificate
  • Identity & Address Proof (Aadhar, PAN)
